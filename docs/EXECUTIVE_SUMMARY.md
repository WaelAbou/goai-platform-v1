# SustainData AI
## Executive Summary

---

### What It Is

An AI-powered platform that **automates carbon tracking and ESG reporting** by reading your documents, calculating emissions, and providing instant insights.

---

### The Problem We Solve

| Pain Point | Impact |
|------------|--------|
| Manual data entry from bills & receipts | 40+ hours/month wasted |
| Scattered data across systems | Incomplete carbon picture |
| Slow quarterly reporting | Missed deadlines, outdated data |
| Calculation errors | Compliance risk |
