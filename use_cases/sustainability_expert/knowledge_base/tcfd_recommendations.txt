# TCFD Recommendations
## Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures

## Overview
The TCFD was established by the Financial Stability Board to develop voluntary, consistent climate-related financial disclosures for companies to provide information to investors, lenders, insurers, and other stakeholders.

## Four Core Elements

### 1. Governance
Disclose the organization's governance around climate-related risks and opportunities.

Recommended Disclosures:
a) Board's oversight of climate-related risks and opportunities
b) Management's role in assessing and managing climate-related risks and opportunities

Key Questions:
- Does the board oversee climate-related issues?
- How often is the board informed about climate issues?
- Who is responsible for climate matters at management level?

### 2. Strategy
Disclose actual and potential impacts of climate-related risks and opportunities on the organization's businesses, strategy, and financial planning.

Recommended Disclosures:
a) Climate-related risks and opportunities identified over short, medium, and long term
b) Impact on the organization's businesses, strategy, and financial planning
c) Resilience of strategy under different climate scenarios (including 2째C or lower)

Time Horizons:
- Short-term: 0-1 years
- Medium-term: 1-5 years
- Long-term: 5-30 years

### 3. Risk Management
Disclose how the organization identifies, assesses, and manages climate-related risks.

Recommended Disclosures:
a) Processes for identifying and assessing climate-related risks
b) Processes for managing climate-related risks
c) Integration into overall risk management

Risk Categories:
- Physical Risks: Acute (extreme weather) and Chronic (sea level rise, temperature changes)
- Transition Risks: Policy, Legal, Technology, Market, Reputation

### 4. Metrics and Targets
Disclose the metrics and targets used to assess and manage climate-related risks and opportunities.

Recommended Disclosures:
a) Metrics used to assess climate-related risks and opportunities
b) Scope 1, Scope 2, and (if appropriate) Scope 3 GHG emissions
c) Targets used to manage climate-related risks and opportunities

Key Metrics:
- GHG emissions (all three scopes)
- Carbon intensity ratios
- Water usage
- Energy usage and mix
- Revenue from low-carbon products

## Scenario Analysis
Organizations should describe the resilience of their strategy under different scenarios:

1. Paris-aligned scenario (1.5째C - 2째C)
2. Current policies scenario (3째C+)
3. Delayed transition scenario

## Implementation Guidance
1. Start with governance structure
2. Integrate climate into enterprise risk management
3. Conduct scenario analysis
4. Set measurable targets
5. Report annually with improving disclosure quality

## Sector-Specific Guidance
TCFD provides supplemental guidance for:
- Financial Sector (Banks, Insurance, Asset Owners, Asset Managers)
- Non-Financial Sectors (Energy, Transportation, Materials & Buildings, Agriculture, Food, Forest Products)
